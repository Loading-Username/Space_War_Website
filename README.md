# Derek's Space War!
## What is Derek's Space War?
Long ago, long before your time, there was a planet called Earth. game captures the legendary battle for Earth that we know today, in the year 2754. In the real world, Earth was wiped from existence forever. Now, you have a chance to change the fate of Earth. Use your fighter pilot to fend off the invaders that destroyed Earth so long ago, and help this beautiful planet stay alive forever, so maybe one day they could be our allies. One day.
